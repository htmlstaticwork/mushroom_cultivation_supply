Rooted in the Science

From a small home laboratory to a national leader in mycology and mushroom growth.

" We believe every grower deserves commercial-grade genetics."

Founded in 2018, Shroom began with a simple observation: the tools available to home cultivators were often unreliable, prone to contamination, and lacked the genetic vigor required for high-yield harvests.

We built our Class 100 cleanroom to bridge this gap. By applying industrial-grade sterilization techniques and rigorous genetic isolation to gourmet and functional mushroom strains, we've empowered a community of over 50,000 growers to succeed from their very first flush.
